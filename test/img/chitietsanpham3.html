4.HYPERDUNK 2017 :


The Nike Hyperdunk 2017 is the ninth Nike Hyperdunk model. It is a high-performance basketball shoe that features a slip-on construction with a mix of either Flyknit or mesh textile over REACT foam cushioning. Currently, there are three models: The Nike Hyperdunk 2017 Low ($130), Nike Hyperdunk 2017 ($140) and Nike Hyperdunk 2017 Flyknit ($160).
